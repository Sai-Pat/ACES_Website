Freshers' Party Report

Department of Computer Engineering
Association of Computer Engineering Students (ACES)
Event Date: 31st July 2025 | Venue: Dronagiri Hall

Event Overview

The Department of Computer Engineering and the Association of Computer Engineering Students (ACES) successfully organized the Freshers’ Party for S.E. students on 31st July 2025 at Dronagiri Hall. The event was filled with excitement, laughter, and celebration as the new batch of students was warmly welcomed through performances, games, and the highly anticipated Mr. & Ms. Fresher contest. This well-coordinated event created lasting memories for all attendees and highlighted the enthusiastic efforts of the ACES student team.

Mr. & Ms. Fresher Contest

The highlight of the evening was the prestigious Mr. & Ms. Fresher Contest, featuring multiple rounds designed to showcase personality, talent, and confidence.

Light-hearted comedy performances and dramatic Walks that brought laughter and joy, creating memorable moments and strengthening bonds among participants.

Felicitation of Seniors

As a mark of respect and appreciation, a special felicitation ceremony was held to honor our seniors, the former ACES coordinators of the academic year 2024–25. These individuals were the backbone of many successful events in the past and have left behind a legacy of leadership, dedication, and excellence. During the event, each senior was warmly welcomed on stage and presented with a token of appreciation — a personalized certificate acknowledging their contribution and commitment to the ACES committee. This small gesture symbolized the immense value they brought to the department and to us as juniors. ACES is not just a committee, it's a family that celebrates every chapter of student life.

Audience Engagement

The heart of any successful event lies in the energy of its audience — and this year's Freshers' Party was a shining example of that. From the very first moment, the hall was buzzing with excitement, laughter, and cheers, as students wholeheartedly immersed themselves in every performance, activity, and announcement. The anchors played a vital role in keeping the momentum alive. With their witty one-liners, relatable jokes, fun shayaris, and spontaneous interactions, they ensured that not a single moment felt dull or disconnected. Whether it was cheering for a performer, laughing at a playful roast, or participating in on-the-spot games, the audience remained vibrantly involved throughout the event.

Digital Coupon System

This year, a QR-based food coupon system was introduced for the first time. Students received digital QR codes via a Google Form, which were scanned at the food counter ensuring quick, organized, and paperless distribution. The system was well-received and added a modern touch to the event. The Food Management Committee ensured hygiene, quality, and smooth service throughout.
