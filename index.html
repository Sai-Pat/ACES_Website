Why ACES?

At the Association of Computer Engineering Students (ACES), Amrutvahini College of Engineering, we believe a committee should be as dynamic as its members. We approach coding with precision, solve problems with curiosity, and celebrate achievements with the same joy as a perfect first compile. Our enthusiasm goes beyond technology — from sports fields to cultural stages, we bring energy, teamwork, and creativity to every endeavor. Whether organizing hackathons, leading technical projects, or managing campus events, ACES is where innovation meets collaboration, making us a driving force in both the department and the college community.

Gratitude

We express our deepest gratitude to Dr. S. K. Sonkar, Head of Department, and Dr. G. D. Puri, ACES Coordinator of the Department of Computer Engineering, Amrutvahini College of Engineering.

Their visionary leadership, constant encouragement, and dedicated mentorship have been the driving force behind ACES. With their guidance, we have been able to foster a culture of innovation, collaboration, and excellence, empowering students to grow not only as skilled engineers but as responsible professionals.
